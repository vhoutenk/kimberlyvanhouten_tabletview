Bus Stop Design Challenge

A SUSTAINABLE AND ACCESSIBLE SHELTER

Winter 2019 | Taubman College Design Challenge

The challenge of the project was to create a bus stop that serves the needs of the community. This design for Holland, MI focuses on inclusivity and sustainability. There is wheelchair accessibility and the audio within the computer systems supports visually impaired persons. The solar panel above the bike rack powers the digital interface and the infrared space heater for cold weather. The attached bike rack promotes a more sustainable means of transportation throughout the community. The form resembles a wave which celebrates the lakeshore of the city of Holland
